The Project

Grown slow.
Documented obsessively.

Zombie Genetix is a personal craft breeding project operating under a Health Canada medical cultivation licence in Carleton Place, Ontario. The focus is CBD-dominant F1 crosses developed in organic living soil — no synthetics, no shortcuts. This site is the paper trail.

Living Soil — Gaia Green amendments, Pure Life media, Silicate reservoir
CinnaBar Dust — CBD Runtz × Red Toba · F1 in development
Brujo Dulce — CBD Runtz × Purple Mexican · F1 in development
No sales. Genetics are documented here for transparency, not commerce.
